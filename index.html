About

Engineer → APM → PM → Founder

I started as an engineer at Eaton, shipping configuration SaaS tools that drove $100M+ in downstream revenue — and realised I cared more about why we built things than how. That pull took me to Truecaller, where I grew from product intern to owning Business IM, one of the company's fastest-growing enterprise revenue lines.

At Truecaller I built the unglamorous-but-critical layers of enterprise messaging: webhook-based event APIs, self-serve partner portals, AI personalization engines, and trust guardrails across business units. I've launched features serving 14M+ messages daily and onboarded partners across India and rest-of-world markets.

Today I'm building Vyn AI — a voice AI sales agent for mid-size Indian B2B businesses — and making AI accessible to Indian audiences through @simplifyingai.
